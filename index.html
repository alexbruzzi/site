To summarize, we do the following

  • Storage of all your analytics data, your data, you own it.
  • Pre-built dashboard to view your data, extend it for your needs!
  • Use own internal SDK (octo.ai based) for all analytics needs, one SDK to rule them all.
  • Continue to use multiple analytics backend (GA, mix panel, etc.), switch them on and off whenever you want.
  • All of this for free!

The backstory

Octo started as off as a platform for fully automating your internet marketing needs. Initially, we were offering intelligent push notifications and personalized feeds (along with the usual dashboard, segmentation, etc.) . You can view the older site here for purely academic/reference purposes :).

Over the few months of building and selling, we have understood that the some of the assumptions we made were flawed. The critical assumption that data would be available to run algorithms was the primary one (the other was a buy in against other analytics players to be around in the stack long enough to gather data).

Present

What we figured was the biggest problem with any form of machine learning or data based system is that we require high quality historic data. When a company starts the heavy dependance on free/freemium analytics services it means that all that data is usually lost for good.

We believe that everyone (yes, you that small startup on its way to glory) should retain the data generated as it is valuable. What we present here is a framework which collects all your analytics data, connects with the free/paid services that always lock in your data and allow you to actually do real data science going forward.
